# Build an AI-Powered Digital Twin of India's Climate

## Project Overview

Build a full-stack AI-powered web application called **ClimateTwin India**.

The platform should create a Digital Twin of India's climate using national climate datasets. The system should simulate weather and climate patterns, predict future scenarios using AI models, visualize the data on interactive maps, and provide decision-support tools for policymakers, researchers, farmers, and disaster management authorities.

The UI should look modern and futuristic (similar to NASA, Windy, Google Earth Engine, and IBM Weather dashboards).
